ACO TimeLine

May 5th, 2015

ACO Starts operations as AEM (the old name for ACO) with the first website called the A Blog. During the same time we were working on Lazo Entertianment which later became the entertainment branch of ACO. The name AEM use to me Around Earth Manegment

September 5th, 2015

We started operations of Lazo, the first website being called Lazo Way. The name Lazo was picked as it was zappy, snappy, and something new!

December 11th, 2015

At this time Lazo has been going well and had overtook A Blog news site and became the main operation, at one point we almost decided to change A Blog to Lazo News but later kept the old name. Lazo was also a website with games, animated videos, and even movies made by Lazo.

Jan 5th, 2016

AEM was 8 months old and we were looking for names, we had a few options in mind like Ayaan WebOperations, Ayaan Website Company, The Ayaan Website, and A Web. We finaly decided on the name A Web and started a seprate website called ACO Video, it was a video charing app where users could upload videos and share them. After a few months of testing we decided to pull out and go with adding more games.

Jan 20th, 2016

A Web had hit 50 users and we made a special celebration website that still lasts to this day, whenever there is an event we decorate it and make it so people recognise the day. We also started the ACO Numbers website which is also running to date counting the number of users. (https://number.smb.company)

Jan 25th, 2016

A Web hit 55 users (just added that in there (55 is a cool number))

May 5th, 2016

A Web was now 1 year old and we went through another rebrand where we considered names link AWebsites, A Company, A Webers, A Website Company, and Ayaan Company. We selected Ayaan Company but made that the FULL NAME, we branded everything with A. CO. Overtime people started to refer to it as ACO and without the dots it also looked more neat, so we startedbranding with ACO.

May 5th, 2017

After a year with no new products and some growth ACO was at 66 users and was constantly updating the current products.

May 5th, 2018

Another year but with 1 new product, ACO Animations, and a milestone of 70 users ACO started its plans for ACO AI, now known as SMARTi App.

December 3rd, 2018

ACO AI was operational brining in many users to ACO getting us to 85 strong.

Jan 17th, 2019

ACO had started pushing out features link mad on ACO AI with some even calling it "Smarter than anything before." ACO AI was state of the art and could beat anything any day. At this point ACO had also developed side apps like ACO Calculator to power the calculation feature of ACO AI. This is the motive behind ACO Search and many other products.

May 5th, 2019

ACO had hit 100 users on its 5th birthday and about another 50 were using the app without making an account. This product also really was popular on news in Japan and the United States.

December 18th, 2019

We have hit 200 users and 400 without an account for a total of 600, at this point ACO AI was kicking the market in its face and taking over the market of small apps.

Jan 21st, 2020

ACO at the time was being hosted on a third party who did not allow us to share its name but had a User Trial And Demo unit on Discord. This Trial And Demo Unit was what brings in 14% of ACO users. Discord who has very bad security had a DDOS attack on thier database which caused a total loss of 200 users on ACO.

Feb 18,19,21,24,26,27,28, 2020

Was filed with issues where Discord kept getting hacked and kept taking us down with it (Life lesson to ANYONE: Never trust discord, they are the worst with security) We were took down all the way to about 10 users due to this.

May 5th, 2020

ACO at this time had about 300 users and had started new products like ACO Meet, ACO Chat, Mail, Support Systems, and basicaly every product that was launched in 2020. We had also left Discord and partnered with a new hosting company to have 1,000 exlusixe server units other over 5 million tb or storage space. ( ACO turned 5)

June 5th, 2020

Operations booming, ACO AI became SMARTi App, and uver count hit 500, ACO was done with our product launch segment of the year and went into bug fixing and update mode. At the end on 2020 we had a total of 50 products, 75 services, and 33 premium users.

Sept 13-14, 2020

ACO was in its first confrence of its 5 year history @ Step Confrence Saudi Arabia that was held online due to COVID-19 (A pandemic during the time)

Jan 1st, 2021

Starting ACO's 6th year in operation with a total 25 new products in the production room and potention enterprise orders from some big companies in the works!

Jan 2nd, 2021

ACO Gets legal for the first time promoting its terms of service and privacy policy!

Jan 3rd, 2021

ACO Starts rapid growth! We grow by 1,000 users per day till the time of writing.
